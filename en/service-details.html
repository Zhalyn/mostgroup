Production of All types Ready-mixed Concrete

Currently, our company operates two state-of-the-art concrete plants, allowing us to promptly and efficiently organize the delivery of high-quality concrete to any point in the region. Thanks to the use of advanced technologies and the availability of a modern fleet of concrete mixers, we guarantee a high level of service and excellent quality of our products. Our plants have large capacities and are capable of producing commercial concrete of any grade using various additives, enabling us to create concrete with the required characteristics.

We produce concrete mixes of the following grades:

  • M100 (B7.5) - ideal for small architectural forms and lightly loaded structures.
  • M150 (B10) - used for foundations and other load-bearing elements of buildings.
  • M200 (B15) - excellent for creating monolithic structures and underground works.
  • M250 (B20) - used for the construction of load-bearing walls, columns, and beams.
  • M300 (B22.5) - suitable for the construction of large engineering and transportation structures.
  • M350 (B25) - ideal for the construction of high-rise buildings and other structures requiring increased strength.
  • M400 (B30) - applied in the construction of objects with special requirements for strength and durability.
